TrackFlow Demo

This demo page is used to test the TrackFlow tracking SDK. A page_view event is automatically recorded when the page loads. Every click on buttons or content areas is tracked and sent to the backend for session analytics and heatmap visualization.

How to Test

  1. 1. Open this demo page.
  2. 2. Click the buttons and different areas above.
  3. 3. A page_view event is automatically tracked.
  4. 4. Every click generates a click event with x/y coordinates.
  5. 5. Open the analytics dashboard.
  6. 6. Refresh the dashboard to view recorded sessions.
  7. 7. Open Heatmap View to see click positions.
